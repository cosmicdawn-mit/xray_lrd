  • Below in this page, for each band, the followings are shown, from left to right: (1) a post-stamped stacked image with a link to the fits version, (2) a bootstrap histogram, (3) the mean count rate (CR) of the accepted objects +/- photon counting statistics error and the mean CR values corresponding to five bootstrap percentiles for the accepted objects, and (4) links to a few analysis products (see the manual).
